a running scratchpad — not a polished post, just things i want to remember.

- `defer` runs in reverse order of declaration, same as most languages with the concept.
- comptime is genuinely just running zig at compile time — no separate macro language to learn.
- allocator interfaces are passed explicitly everywhere, which feels verbose at first and then feels obvious.
